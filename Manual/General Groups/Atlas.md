## Atlas
Group for atlas texture mapping.

---
## Inputs

- **Frame Count**
	- The count of horizontal rows on the atlas.
- **Row Count**
	- The count of vertical rows on the atlas.
- **Frame**
	- Used to select a frame.
- **Row**
	- Used to select a row.

## Outputs 

- **Vector**
	- Connect to texture vector inputs.
