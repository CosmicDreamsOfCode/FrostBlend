## Pupil Scale 
Handles scaling of pupils.

---
## Inputs

- **Pupil Scale**
	- Simple uniform scale value.
- **A**
	- DICE eye A texture input.
- **Vector**
	- Iris Scale output connects here.

## Outputs 

- **DA Vector**
	- Connect to DA/CA texture vector input.
- **A**
	- Connect to DICE Eyes group A input.
